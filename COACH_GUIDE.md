# Coach App — How-To Guide

---

## 1. First Time Setup — My Profile

Before you send your first invoice, fill in your profile by tapping **Profile**. This information appears at the top of every invoice you send.

---

## 2. Add Students

Tap **Students** in the bottom navigation bar. Once you add students, you can use the **Search** bar at the top to filter by name. Tap any student to open their profile.

---

## 3. Add a Charge

Use this page to log a lesson or any other charge. Multiple students can be included in the charge (ex: group lessons). Upon save, the event will automatically appear on your calendar.

---

## 4. Calendar

Tap **Calendar** in the bottom navigation bar to see all your scheduled events.

### Navigating the Calendar
You can edit / delete events by tapping on them.
Events are **colour-coded** by type — tap **Colour Legend ▾** at the bottom to see what each colour means.

### Exporting Your Schedule
You can export your calendar at any time as a backup by tapping the **Export** button in the calendar toolbar. Choose your format:
   - **Download PDF Schedule** — a clean, printable list of all your lessons organized by month, including student names, times, durations, lesson types, and notes. Great for printing or saving as a record.
   - **Download .ics (Google / Apple Calendar)** — a standard calendar file that can be imported into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, or any other calendar app.

---

## 5. Invoices

Tap **Invoices** in the bottom navigation bar to generate and send invoices.

### Generating an Invoice
**Search for a student** by typing their name. Their charges are automatically pulled in from your calendar.

### Reviewing Line Items
- Each lesson appears as a card. Tap a card to expand it and see full details (time, duration, rate, notes).
- To remove a lesson from this invoice, expand it and tap **Skip from Invoice**. It will appear faded with a "—" amount. Tap **Include in Invoice** to add it back.

### Adding Custom Charges
Scroll to the **Custom Charges** section to add one-off items not in your calendar.

### HST and Previous Balance
- Toggle **Apply HST (13%)** to add tax to the subtotal.
- If the student has an outstanding balance or credit, a toggle appears to include it in the invoice automatically.

### Sending the Invoice
You can **Preview PDF** to review the invoice before sending. Tap **Send Invoice via Gmail** to email it directly to the student.

---

## Tips

- **Recurring charges** you set up under Add a Charge will automatically populate the Calendar and Invoices every week — you don't have to log them manually each time.
- **Notes** added to a lesson (e.g. "Worked on double axel") appear on the invoice so the student can see what was covered.
- Use **Export → Download PDF** at the start of each season as a backup of your full schedule in case anything needs to be recovered.
- The **Billing Name** on a student's profile lets you address invoices to a parent or guardian rather than the student.
